World Covid-19 Vaccination Data (April 10, 2021)

The following map is a visual representaion of the most recent Covid-19 data throughout the world. This map contains three distinct datasets:

1. Total Vaccinations (Number of people per hundred that have received at least one shot of the vaccine.)

2. New Vaccinations (Number of new people per million vaccinated in the past seven days.)

3. People Fully Vaccinated (Number of people per hundred that have been fully vaccinated.)

Author: Alexander Ivanov | Data Sources: Natural Earth, Our World in Data
